Why We're Building This

Platforms like Vercel are amazing for prototypes. Generous free tiers. One-click deploys. They made frontend deployment accessible.

But they're built on a bandwidth trap. They hook you with free deployments, then charge exponentially when you scale. Success becomes punishment.

We're indie developers too. We've been burned by surprise bills. We've seen projects shut down because of traffic spikes. We've watched developers choose between going viral and going bankrupt.

CDCat exists to fix this. Deploy static sites, React apps, or Next.js exports without worrying about your next bill. Flat pricing. No hidden fees. No egress charges.
